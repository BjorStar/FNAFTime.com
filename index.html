About Freddy Fazbear’s Pizzeria

Welcome to the home of cheesy slices, arcade blips, and the friendliest animatronics in town. We’re the neighborhood spot where birthdays turn into core memories, parents get a breather, and kids leave grinning ear to ear.

Our story

Freddy Fazbear’s Pizzeria started with a simple idea: give families a place where imagination feels real. From the first sing‑along with Freddy to the countless candles blown out on stage, we’ve grown into a beloved community hub that mixes classic pizza‑parlor charm with an unforgettable show experience.

What we offer

-Fresh, family‑friendly menu: Hand‑tossed pizzas, crispy sides, salads, and sweet treats—made for sharing.

-Live animatronic shows: Freddy and friends perform throughout the day with music, jokes, and crowd participation.

-Arcade and prizes: Retro favorites and new hits, plus prize counters that make winning feel extra rewarding.

-Birthday packages: Stage shout‑outs, themed decor, and dedicated party hosts to keep everything running smoothly.

Meet the band

-Freddy Fazbear: The charismatic leader with a golden voice and a heart (and hat) of showtime.

-Bonnie the Bunny: Guitar hero with catchy riffs and high‑energy stage presence.

-Chica the Chicken: Party starter who never forgets the snacks and sing‑along cues.

-Foxy the Pirate: Adventure-loving rogue with fast beats and fan-favorite antics.

Tip: Check the daily schedule near the stage for showtimes and character meet‑and‑greets.

Safety and comfort

Cleanliness first: Regular sanitation of dining areas, arcades, and party rooms throughout the day.

Family-focused design: High chairs, quiet corners, and accessible facilities to make every visit easy.

Friendly staff support: Hosts and attendants ready to help with seating, dietary questions, and game tokens.

Celebrate with us

Party rooms: Themed spaces with flexible seating for small groups or big celebrations.

Personalized moments: Custom cake messages, on-stage birthday shout‑outs, and photo ops with the band.

Easy booking: Simple online reservations and clear package options for stress-free planning.

Visit details

Location: Right where the music’s playing—check our map for the nearest Freddy Fazbear’s Pizzeria.

Hours: Open daily with extended weekend showtimes.

Contact: Reach our team for bookings, special requests, or community events.

Why families choose Freddy’s

Memorable experiences: A place where kids feel like stars and parents feel like guests.

Value that lasts: Good food, good fun, and keepsake moments you’ll talk about on the ride home.

Community spirit: School nights, charity drives, and local partnerships—because we’re more than a pizzeria; we’re part of the neighborhood.

We can’t wait to welcome you. Grab a slice, catch a show, and let Freddy and the gang make your next visit unforgettable.
